## Keyboard Testing

### Why aren't some keys registering?
Some keys may be blocked by the browser or operating system. Try:
1. Using a different browser
2. Disabling browser extensions
3. Testing in incognito mode

Test your keyboard at [keyboardtester.click](https://keyboardtester.click).

## Mouse Testing

### How accurate is the DPI tester?
The [DPI tester](https://keyboardtester.click/mouse_sensitivity_DPI_tester.php) is accurate to within a few DPI when used correctly. Follow the on-screen instructions for best results.

### What is ghost clicking?
Ghost clicking is when your mouse registers clicks you didn't make. Test for this issue with our [Ghost Click Detector](https://keyboardtester.click/ghost-click-detector.php).

### What's a good CPS (clicks per second)?
- Average: 6-8 CPS
- Good: 8-10 CPS
- Excellent: 10+ CPS

Test your speed at [keyboardtester.click/mouse_speed_tester.php](https://keyboardtester.click/mouse_speed_tester.php).

## Audio/Video Testing

### Why can't the site access my microphone?
Make sure you've granted microphone permissions in your browser. Test at [keyboardtester.click/mic-tester.php](https://keyboardtester.click/mic-tester.php).

### My webcam isn't working - what should I do?
1. Check browser permissions
2. Make sure no other app is using the webcam
3. Try refreshing the page

Test your webcam at [keyboardtester.click/webcamtesterindex.php](https://keyboardtester.click/webcamtesterindex.php).
