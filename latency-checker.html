    🔍 Understanding Latency

    Latency is the time (in milliseconds) between when you press a key and when it's registered on-screen. Lower latency is better for responsiveness, especially in gaming or real-time tasks.

    • ✦ Ideal Latency: Below 10ms is excellent for most applications.
    • ✦ Acceptable: 10-20ms is typical for standard keyboards.
    • ✦ High Latency: Above 20ms may indicate delays or hardware issues.

    ❓ FAQs

    • What does this tool measure?
      It measures the time between consecutive key presses in your browser.
    • Why do results vary?
      Browser performance, system load, and keyboard hardware can affect readings.
    • How accurate is it?
      It’s a browser-based estimate, not hardware-level precision, but useful for comparison.

    🔧 Tips for Better Results

    • ✦ Close Apps: Minimize background processes for consistent results.
    • ✦ Test Multiple Times: Average multiple tests for accuracy.
    • ✦ Check Hardware: Ensure your keyboard is in good condition.
